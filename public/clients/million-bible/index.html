The concern David keeps raising
"We place millions of Bibles — but once they leave the press, we lose sight of them."
The Million.Bible model is a network: national Bible societies, churches, publishers and donors, each moving 10,000+ Bibles at a time. That reach is the strength — and exactly why tracking breaks down across spreadsheets, email threads and offline field teams.
🗺️
No single source of truth

Counts live in each partner's own spreadsheet. Pledged, printed, shipped and placed never reconcile.

📡
The last mile is invisible

Placements happen in rural areas with no signal. By the time numbers surface, they're weeks old and unverifiable.

🤝
Donors want proof, not promises

"Where did my Bibles go?" is hard to answer with evidence — which caps trust, retention and larger gifts.

The heart of Passage · two levels of tracking

Two QR codes. One tracks the shipment — one gives every Bible a life of its own.

This is the idea that sets Passage apart. A code on every carton follows the logistics. A unique code inside every single Bible confirms it reached a person — and then stays open for life: a private line to a devotional in their own language, a pastor, and the donor who gave it.

📴
Connectivity — handled up front. The tracking never depends on the field being online: placements are logged offline and sync later. The per-Bible Living Link is a gift for recipients who do have a phone, to open whenever they want. Those who don't simply keep their Bible — nothing to install, nothing to miss.

The field · partner & distributor app

Built for the last mile — even with no signal

A distributor in rural Uganda scans the batch, taps the count, captures GPS, and optionally adds a photo and testimony. It works fully offline and syncs the moment a bar of signal appears.

  • ✓Offline-first. Records save on the phone and reconcile with headquarters later — no connection required at the point of placement.
  • ✓Scan, don't type. A QR label on each carton means a placement is logged in seconds, not on paper to be re-entered weeks later.
  • ✓Proof at the source. GPS, photo and a voice-or-text testimony turn a number into verifiable evidence.
  • ✓Any phone, any partner. Runs in a browser as an installable app — nothing to buy, nothing to maintain in the field.
Donors · the trust engine

"Track the Bibles you gave" — like tracking a parcel

Every gift gets its own page. Donors watch their Bibles move from funded to placed, read a testimony from the community they reached, and download an impact certificate. This is the reporting that turns a one-time gift into a lifetime partner.

  • ✓Radical transparency. The same chain-of-custody data, told as a story the donor can feel.
  • ✓Auto-generated impact reports. Placement data and translated testimonies become donor updates without staff writing them by hand.
  • ✓Feeds PULSE. Every interaction enriches the donor profile your AI roadmap already calls for.
